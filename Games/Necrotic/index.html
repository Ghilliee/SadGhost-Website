WHAT IS NECROTIC?

Necrotic is a intense single player game that combines strategic base building with intense zombie survival gameplay. Players take on the role of the lone survivor,
fighting against hordes of zombies while strategically upgrading their home base to unlock new weapons, abilities and defenses. The game offers a unique blend of base building and survival gameplay,
where players must not only fend off the relentless Necrotic hordes, but also manage resources and upgrade their home base to unlock new features.
Necrotic offers an exciting and satisfying experience for players who are looking for a true test of their survival skills.

ABILITY SYSTEM

In Necrotic, players can unlock and upgrade their abilities by reaching a certain level and then purchasing the ability they want.
Upgrading Abilities allow players to become stronger and fight longer, players must strategize which ability they want to buy first and which one to upgrade next, to increase their chances of survival.

BOSSES

In Necrotic, Bosses come in all shapes and sizes, some can hit you from a far, some can smack you to the side and deal great amounts of damage.
They come in different forms, each with their own strengths, players must upgrade their weapons and abilities in-order to take care of these enemies easier.
